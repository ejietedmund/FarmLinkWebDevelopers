About FarmLink

Our Mission

FarmLink’s mission is to revolutionize livestock trading in Uganda by creating a transparent, efficient marketplace that connects local farmers with buyers, maximizing profits for farmers while ensuring high-quality livestock for consumers.

How FarmLink Works

Register

Create an account to either sell or buy livestock on the platform.

List or Browse

Post livestock for sale or browse available livestock from other farmers.

Connect & Trade

Communicate directly with farmers or buyers, and finalize transactions securely.
