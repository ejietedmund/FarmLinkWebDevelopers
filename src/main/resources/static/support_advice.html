Livestock Farming Tips

Proper Nutrition

Ensure your livestock has a balanced nutrition with adequate protein, energy, vitamins, and minerals.

Clean Water Supply

Always provide clean, fresh water for your animals. A cow can drink up to 50 liters per day.

Regular Health Checks

Monitor animals regularly for signs of illness or disease. Early detection of health issues can prevent outbreaks.

Proper Housing

Keep housing areas hygienic with adequate ventilation, flooring, and protection from extreme weather.

Vaccination Schedule

Follow a recommended vaccination program by your local veterinarian to protect animals from common diseases.

About FarmLink

Our Mission

FarmLink’s mission is to revolutionize livestock trading in Uganda by creating a transparent, efficient marketplace that connects local farmers with buyers, maximizing profits for farmers while ensuring high-quality livestock for consumers.

How FarmLink Works

  1. Register: Create an account to either sell or buy livestock on the platform.
  2. List or Browse: Post livestock for sale or browse available livestock.
  3. Connect & Trade: Communicate directly with farmers or buyers and finalize transactions securely.
